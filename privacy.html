2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Account information: name, email address, and password when you register
  • Profile information: display name, profile photo
  • User-generated content: swipe preferences, restaurant selections, session data

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Location data: precise or approximate GPS location to find nearby restaurants (only when you grant permission)
  • Device information: device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, app version, and advertising identifiers (e.g., Apple IDFA) when you grant permission
  • Usage data: features used, screens viewed, session length, time of day and day of week of usage
  • Swipe analytics: which restaurants you swipe on (left or right), cuisine type, price level, swipe duration, and frequency — used to build your personalized taste profile and improve the service
  • Interaction data: how you engage with sponsored content and advertisements shown in the App
  • Crash reports and diagnostics via Firebase Crashlytics

Information from Third Parties:

  • Google Places API: restaurant data fetched based on your location
  • Firebase (Google LLC): authentication, data storage, analytics, and crash reporting

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App
  • Create and manage your account
  • Match you with restaurant suggestions based on your location and preferences
  • Build a personalized taste profile from your swipe history to improve restaurant recommendations over time
  • Calculate compatibility scores between swiping partners to enhance shared session results
  • Facilitate shared swiping sessions with friends
  • Display relevant advertisements and sponsored content based on your preferences, location, and taste profile (see Section 5 below)
  • Create aggregated, de-identified audience segments that advertisers can target (e.g., "Italian food lovers in Miami") without accessing your individual data
  • Generate anonymized analytics and trend reports for advertising partners (e.g., "65% of users in your area prefer casual dining")
  • Measure ad performance — we track whether ads shown to you were viewed, tapped, or led to a restaurant visit, and share this data with advertisers in aggregated form only
  • Send push notifications (with your permission)
  • Monitor and fix bugs and performance issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your individual personal information to third parties. We never give advertisers access to your name, email, profile, or individual swipe history. Here is how we share information:

  • Service providers: Firebase/Google, Google Places API, and cloud infrastructure providers — only as needed to operate the App
  • Advertising partners: We may share aggregated, de-identified data and audience segment information with advertising partners. This means advertisers can target categories of users (e.g., "people who like sushi in Chicago") but they never receive your name, email, profile photo, or any data that personally identifies you. We serve the ads — advertisers only see anonymized performance metrics (impressions, clicks, conversion rates)
  • Ad measurement partners: We may work with third-party ad measurement and analytics services that help us and our advertising partners measure ad effectiveness. These partners receive only de-identified or aggregated data
  • Other users: your display name and profile photo are visible to friends in a shared session
  • Legal compliance: if required by law, court order, or government authority
  • Business transfers: if the Company is acquired or merges with another entity, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction

To be clear: We operate like major platforms (Meta, Google, etc.) — we build audience segments internally and serve ads on our platform. Advertisers choose segments to target, but your individual data never leaves our systems. The advertiser sees results, not your personal information.

5. Advertising & Sponsored Content

The App may display advertisements and sponsored content from restaurants, food brands, and other businesses. Here's how our advertising works:

How we target ads:

  • We use your taste profile, general location (city/region, not precise GPS), cuisine preferences, price level preferences, and usage patterns to show you ads that are relevant to your interests
  • Advertisers select audience segments (e.g., "pizza lovers within 10 miles of downtown Austin") through our advertising platform. We then match and serve the ad to qualifying users. The advertiser never sees who you are
  • We may also show non-targeted ads that are not based on your personal data

What advertisers receive:

  • Aggregated performance reports: impressions, click-through rates, conversion metrics — all in aggregate, never individual-level
  • Anonymized trend data: cuisine popularity, dining time trends, price preferences by region — no individual user is identifiable
  • NO access to: your name, email, profile photo, individual swipe history, precise location, or any other personally identifiable information

Advertising identifiers: With your permission (via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt on iOS), we may collect your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) to measure ad effectiveness and improve ad relevance. You can deny or revoke this permission at any time through your device settings, and we will not track you across other apps or websites.

Opting out of targeted advertising: You can opt out of personalized ads at any time through the App's privacy settings. If you opt out, you will still see ads, but they will not be tailored to your preferences. See Section 7 for your full opt-out rights.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the App's services. Specifically:

  • Account data: retained while your account is active; deleted within 30 days of an account deletion request
  • Taste profile data: retained while your account is active; you may reset your taste profile at any time
  • Swipe analytics (raw events): retained for up to 18 months, then automatically deleted or anonymized
  • Aggregated/anonymized data: may be retained indefinitely as it cannot be linked back to you
  • Ad interaction data: retained for up to 12 months for measurement purposes, then deleted or anonymized

You may request deletion at any time by using the "Delete Account" option in the App or by contacting flashjellis@gmail.com. We process deletion requests within 30 days.

7. Your Rights & Choices

All Users:

  • Delete your account: Use the "Delete Account" button in the App at any time
  • Opt out of targeted ads: Toggle off personalized advertising in the App's privacy settings
  • Revoke location access: Disable location permissions in your device settings
  • Revoke tracking permission: On iOS, go to Settings → Privacy → Tracking to disable our access to your advertising identifier
  • Reset taste profile: Clear your swipe history and start fresh
  • Disable push notifications: Turn off in your device's notification settings

State-Specific Rights:

We respect privacy rights granted to residents of all U.S. states. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights including:

  • Right to know/access: Request what personal information we have collected about you
  • Right to delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to correct: Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Right to portability: Receive a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Right to opt out of targeted advertising: Opt out of ads personalized to your profile
  • Right to opt out of "sale" or "sharing": Under CCPA/CPRA, using personal information for targeted advertising may constitute "sharing." You have the right to opt out. We honor Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals.
  • Right to non-discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights

These rights apply under California (CCPA/CPRA), Virginia (VCDPA), Colorado (CPA), Connecticut (CTDPA), Texas (TDPSA), Oregon (OCPA), Montana (MCDPA), and all other enacted state privacy laws. Where state law provides greater protections, the state law controls.

To exercise your rights, email flashjellis@gmail.com or use the in-app controls. We respond to verified requests within 45 days (or sooner if required by your state's law). You may appeal a denied request by contacting us.

9. Taste Profiles & Personalization

When you swipe on restaurants, we build a taste profile tied to your account. This profile includes your preferred cuisine types, price level tendencies, swipe patterns, time-of-day preferences, and dining frequency. We use this data to:

  • Rank and sort restaurant suggestions so you see places you're more likely to enjoy
  • Calculate compatibility scores between you and your swiping partner
  • Determine which audience segments you belong to for ad targeting (e.g., "sushi enthusiasts" or "budget-friendly diners")
  • Improve our recommendation algorithms and overall service quality

Your individual taste profile is stored securely in Firebase and is never shared with third parties in identifiable form. Advertisers can target audience segments that your profile may match, but they never see your profile itself. You can reset your taste profile or delete your account at any time.

10. Location Data

We collect your location to find restaurants near you, only while you actively use the App and only with your explicit permission. We use your location to:

  • Show you nearby restaurants
  • Determine your general area (city/region) for location-relevant ads — we use general area only, not your precise GPS coordinates, for advertising purposes

We do not track your location in the background. We do not sell your location data. Revoke location permission anytime in your device settings.
