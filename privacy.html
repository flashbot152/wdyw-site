Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 10, 2026  ·  Last Updated: March 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how What Do You Want? ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application What Do You Want? (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Who We Are

What Do You Want? is operated by Jordan Ellis. Questions? Email us: support@wdyw.io

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Account information: name, email address, and password when you register
  • Profile information: display name, profile photo
  • User-generated content: swipe preferences, restaurant selections, session data

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Location data: precise or approximate GPS location to find nearby restaurants (only when you grant permission)
  • Device information: device type, OS, unique device identifiers, app version
  • Usage data: features used, screens viewed, session length
  • Crash reports and diagnostics via Firebase Crashlytics

Information from Third Parties:

  • Google Places API: restaurant data fetched based on your location
  • Firebase (Google LLC): authentication, data storage, analytics, and crash reporting

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App
  • Create and manage your account
  • Match you with restaurant suggestions based on your location and preferences
  • Facilitate shared swiping sessions with friends
  • Send push notifications (with your permission)
  • Monitor and fix bugs and performance issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers: Firebase/Google and Google Places API — only as needed to operate the App
  • Other users: your display name and profile photo are visible to friends in a shared session
  • Legal compliance: if required by law, court order, or government authority
  • Business transfers: if the Company is acquired or merges with another entity

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting support@wdyw.io. We process deletion requests within 30 days.

6. Your Rights by State

We respect privacy rights granted to residents of all U.S. states. Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data, and to opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data).

This includes rights under California (CCPA/CPRA), Virginia (VCDPA), Colorado (CPA), Connecticut (CTDPA), Texas (TDPSA), and all other enacted state privacy laws. To exercise your rights, contact support@wdyw.io. We respond within 45 days.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Location Data

We collect your location to find restaurants near you, only while you actively use the App and only with your permission. We do not track your location in the background. Revoke location permission anytime in your device settings.

9. Push Notifications

We may send push notifications about matches and session invites. Opt out anytime through your device notification settings.

10. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. No system is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately.
